Transmisores de Temperatura

Transmisor de temperatura

YTA610
Temperature Transmitter

The YTA610 is the highly accurate temperature transmitter that accepts Thermocouple, RTD, ohms or DC millivolts inputs and converts it to a 4 to 20 mA DC or Fieldbus signal for transmission.
The YTA610 supports HART and FOUNDATION Fieldbus communication protocols.
HART type is certifi ed as complying with SIL 2 for safety requirement.

Informacion Detallada
  • Descripción

Fabricante: YOKOGAWA
Descripción: Transmisor de temperatura
Sensor: RTD (2, 3 y 4 hilos), Termocupla, ohms o milivolts DC
Comunicación: 4 a 20 mA, HART, Profibus, Foundation Fieldbus
Rango: sujeto a rango sensor. Para Pt-100: -200 – 850 °C
Span Minimo:
• RTD: 25°C
• Termocupla: 10°C
Precisión: sujeta a elemento sensor y span configurado
Para Pt-100, con span de 200°C: ± 0.1 °C
Certificación: IECEx, Factory Mutual, ATEX, otras
Opcional: Wireless ISA-100, RTD, Termocupla, Termovaina
