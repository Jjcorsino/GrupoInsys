Transmisor de Nivel - Sensor Remoto Diferencial

EJXC40A
Digital Remote Sensor

EJXC40A Digital Remote Sensor (DRS) Transmitter connects two pressure sensors, master (high pressure side) and slave (low pressure side) in a remote location, with DRS dedicated communication cable to measure differential pressure. EJXC40A is suitable to measure liquid, gas, or steam flow as well as liquid level, density and pressure and outputs a 4 to 20 mA DC signal corresponding to the measured differential pressure. Monitoring or parameter setting can be remotely done via HART communication.
